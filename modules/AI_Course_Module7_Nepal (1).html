Lesson 1 · The Foundation
The RACE Framework: Never Write a Weak Prompt Again
⏱ 20 minutes Core Skill High Impact
01
🎯
The Big Idea
You have been talking to AI like asking a stranger for directions — vague, incomplete, hoping they understand. After this lesson, you will give AI instructions like a professional briefing an assistant — specific, complete, and structured. The difference in results is enormous.
📖
The Lesson

Imagine you walk into a restaurant and say: "Give me food." The waiter is confused. Do you want dal bhat or mo:mo? Spicy or mild? Vegetarian? Now imagine you say: "I'd like a plate of buff sekuwa — medium spice, with achar on the side, and a glass of lassi." You get exactly what you want.

AI prompting works the same way. The more specific and structured your instructions, the better the results. This is where most people go wrong — they use AI like a search engine, typing 3 words, and then get frustrated when the answer is mediocre.

The RACE Framework — Your Prompt Formula

Every great prompt has four parts. You do not always need all four — but the more you include, the better your results.

⚡ The RACE Framework — 4 Parts of a Perfect Prompt
R
Role — Tell AI who to BE
You are an expert Nepali marketing consultant who has helped small businesses in Kathmandu grow their social media. → Now AI will answer from that expertise
A
Action — Tell AI exactly what to DO
Write 5 Facebook post ideas for Dashain season that will make people want to buy handicrafts as gifts. → Specific, clear task
C
Context — Give background information
My shop is Patan Handicrafts, we sell Thangka paintings and pashmina shawls, our customers are mostly tourists and diaspora Nepalis abroad. → AI now understands YOUR situation
E
Expectation — Describe the FORMAT you want
Each post should be under 100 words, include 3 relevant emojis, and end with a call to action. Write in a warm, friendly tone — not too formal. → Removes all guesswork

Weak Prompt vs. RACE Prompt — See the Difference

❌ Weak Prompt

"Write a Facebook post for my shop"

Result

Generic, bland post that could be for any shop anywhere in the world. You will have to rewrite it completely.

✓ RACE Prompt

"You are an expert social media manager for Nepali businesses. Write a Dashain Facebook post for my pashmina shawl shop in Patan. My customers are Nepali diaspora abroad who miss home. Make them feel nostalgic and excited to buy a gift for their family. Under 120 words, warm tone, end with a clear link invitation."

Result

Emotionally compelling post that speaks directly to your audience's homesickness — ready to post with minimal editing.

💡
The Golden Rule of Prompting You would not hire a new assistant and give them zero information. You would explain who you are, what you need, who your customers are, and how you want it done. Treat AI the same way — it performs best when it knows your full situation.

Lesson 2 · Persona Power
Role Prompting: Make AI Be Any Expert You Need
⏱ 22 minutes Advanced Technique Money Saver
02
🎭
The Big Idea
What if you could hire a lawyer, a marketing expert, a financial advisor, and a writing coach — all for free, available 24 hours a day? With role prompting, you can. You tell AI to step into a specific expert's shoes, and it responds with that expertise. This one technique alone is worth thousands of rupees in saved consulting fees.
📖
The Lesson

Think about the difference between asking a random passerby on the street "How do I grow my business?" versus asking a successful entrepreneur who has built 10 companies the same question. The answer is completely different — not because the question changed, but because the person answering changed.

Role prompting works on the same principle. When you tell AI "You are an expert in X," it shifts its entire approach to answer from that expert's perspective — using that expert's vocabulary, frameworks, and priorities.

Powerful Roles to Use in Your Business

⚖️
Business Lawyer
Use for: contract language, terms & conditions, understanding your rights, drafting agreements.
"You are an experienced business lawyer in Nepal who knows Nepali business law. Help me understand..."
📊
Marketing Strategist
Use for: campaign ideas, pricing psychology, customer messages, festival promotions.
"You are a marketing expert specializing in small businesses in South Asia. My problem is..."
💰
Financial Advisor
Use for: pricing your products, understanding costs, planning for lean seasons, saving strategies.
"You are a financial advisor who helps small business owners understand their numbers..."
✍️
Copywriter
Use for: product descriptions, social media captions, sales messages, website text.
"You are a world-class copywriter who writes emotional, persuasive product descriptions..."

The Advanced Role Prompt Formula

A basic role: "You are a marketing expert." → Fine, but generic.

An advanced role adds three things: the specific type of expert, their experience level, and their specialization relevant to you. Like this:

ADVANCED ROLE PROMPT EXAMPLE
# Basic role (weak)
"You are a marketing expert. Write me a tagline."
# Advanced role (powerful)
"You are a senior marketing strategist with 15 years of experience building brands for tourism and hospitality businesses in South Asia. You understand Nepali culture deeply — the importance of festivals, family values, and trust in business relationships. My business is a family-run trekking agency in Pokhara called 'Himalayan Roots Treks'. We specialize in authentic cultural treks, not just mountain views. Our ideal customer is an international traveler aged 30-55 who wants a meaningful experience, not just a selfie. Please write 5 possible taglines for our agency. Make each one emotionally compelling and different in approach."
Why This Works
The AI now has a complete picture of who it is, who you are, and what success looks like. The output will be dramatically more relevant and professional.
⚠️
Important Reminder AI is not a real lawyer, doctor, or certified financial advisor. For truly important decisions — legal contracts, medical issues, major financial investments — always verify with a qualified professional. Think of AI as a knowledgeable friend who helps you prepare better questions and understand your situation — not the final authority.

Lesson 3 · Chain Prompting
Chain Prompting: Build Big Results Step by Step
⏱ 24 minutes Advanced Technique Creative Power
03
🔗
The Big Idea
Most people try to get everything from AI in one giant prompt. This is like asking your carpenter to design, build, paint, and deliver furniture all at the same time with one instruction. Better approach: work in steps. Each AI response becomes the foundation for the next request. This is called chain prompting — and it is how professionals use AI to create truly impressive work.
📖
The Lesson

Think about how you cook a complex Nepali meal. You do not throw all the ingredients into the pot at once. You make the masala first, then fry it, then add the vegetables in the right order, then adjust the seasoning at the end. Each step builds on the last.

Chain prompting works exactly like this. You give AI one focused task. Review the output. Then give the next task that builds on what AI just created. The final result is far superior to anything you could get from one prompt.

A Real Example: Creating a Full Festival Campaign in 5 Steps

1
Step 1: Generate Ideas
Ask AI: "Give me 10 Tihar festival marketing campaign ideas for a clothing shop targeting young Nepali women aged 18-30 in Kathmandu."
→ AI generates 10 ideas. You pick your favourite 2.
2
Step 2: Develop One Idea
Ask AI: "I like idea #3. Let's develop it. Write a full 30-day content calendar around the 'Gift of Light' concept for October-November. Include one post per day — theme and caption outline."
→ AI creates the calendar. You review and adjust dates/themes.
3
Step 3: Write the Key Posts
Ask AI: "Now write the full caption for the 5 most important days in this calendar — including the launch day, mid-campaign, and the final offer day. Each caption should be under 150 words and end with a WhatsApp call to action."
→ AI writes 5 polished captions. You personalise with your shop name and offers.
4
Step 4: Create Matching Messages
Ask AI: "Based on this campaign, write 3 WhatsApp broadcast messages to send to my existing customers — one teaser, one launch day, one last-chance reminder. Keep each under 80 words."
→ AI writes your customer messages. Ready to send with one copy-paste.
5
Step 5: Refine & Polish
Ask AI: "Review everything we created together. Are there any inconsistencies in tone or message? What would make this campaign stronger? Suggest 3 improvements."
→ AI does a quality check on its own work. You have a campaign a professional agency would charge NPR 50,000 to create.
🧠
The Key to Chain Prompting AI remembers everything in your current conversation. Use this. Refer back to what it already created. Say "Based on what you wrote above..." or "Now take the campaign you just created and..." This is what makes chain prompting so powerful — each step gets smarter and more tailored.
🔗
Task 3: Your 3-Step Chain

Choose one of these chain prompting projects for your business:

  1. Option A: Generate product ideas → Write descriptions → Create Instagram captions for each
  2. Option B: Brainstorm service packages → Price and describe each → Write a menu/list to share with customers
  3. Option C: Create a festival campaign → Write 3 posts → Draft a WhatsApp broadcast message

Work through all 3 steps in a single ChatGPT conversation. Notice how each step builds on the last — the final output will be much more sophisticated than anything you could have asked for in one go.

🎯 Chain prompting is what separates AI beginners from AI professionals. You just became a professional.

Simple AI Automation: Let AI Work While You Sleep
⏱ 24 minutes Time Saver Future Skill
04
⚡
The Big Idea
Automation sounds complicated. It is not. In this lesson you will learn three simple ways to make AI do repetitive work automatically — without any coding, without any technical degree, and without spending money. These techniques work on your phone or computer, starting today.
📖
The Lesson

Automation is not about robots taking over. It is about setting up systems so that repetitive tasks happen quickly — either automatically or with very little effort from you.

Think about the tasks you do over and over: replying to common customer questions, posting on social media, updating your product listings, translating messages. What if each of these took 30 seconds instead of 10 minutes?

3 Simple Automation Techniques — No Coding Needed

1
📋 Saved Prompt Templates (The "Instant Answer" System)
Create a document (Notes app, Google Docs, or even a notebook) with your 10 most-used prompts ready to copy-paste. Customer inquiry reply? Copy, paste, run. Weekly social post? Copy, paste, update the product name, run. This turns a 15-minute task into a 90-second task.
2
🗂️ ChatGPT Custom Instructions (Your Personal Setup)
In ChatGPT settings, you can save information about yourself so you never have to repeat it. Add your business name, your customers, your preferred language and tone. Every new conversation automatically knows who you are. Setup time: 10 minutes. Time saved forever: every single conversation.
3
⏰ The Weekly AI Planning Session (Batch Your Work)
Reserve 1–2 hours every Monday morning. In one AI session: create all your week's social posts, draft the week's customer follow-up messages, plan any new content, answer business questions. "Batching" your AI work means you are in focus mode — more creative, faster, and the results are more consistent because AI remembers your context across the whole session.

Setting Up ChatGPT Custom Instructions (Step by Step)

HOW TO SET UP CUSTOM INSTRUCTIONS
# Step 1: Open ChatGPT → Click your profile picture (top right) → Settings
# Step 2: Find "Personalization" → "Custom Instructions"
# Step 3: Fill in the first box — "What should ChatGPT know about you?"
Example to paste: My name is [Your Name]. I run [Business Name], a [type of business] in [city], Nepal. My customers are [description]. I primarily communicate in [language]. My business tone is warm, friendly, and trustworthy. Dashain, Tihar, and trekking season are my most important business periods. I sell [your products/services].
# Step 4: Fill in the second box — "How should ChatGPT respond to you?"
Example to paste: Always give practical, actionable advice. Keep language simple and clear — avoid overly technical terms. When writing content, match the warm and friendly tone of my business. Always consider the Nepali cultural context — festivals, family values, community trust. Format responses with clear headings when the answer is long.
✅ Result After Setup
Every new ChatGPT conversation now starts with full knowledge of who you are. You never have to re-explain your business. AI becomes a true personal assistant that knows your context.

Common Mistakes to Avoid
✗
Trying to automate everything at once
Start with the one task that wastes the most time. Master that. Then add more. Trying to change everything at once leads to confusion and giving up.
✗
Using AI outputs without personalizing
Even with perfect prompts, AI creates a first draft. Your job is to add the 10% that makes it truly yours — your local reference, your personal touch, your specific offer. Never post AI content without reading it first.
✗
Giving up after one bad response
If the response is not good, improve the prompt — do not abandon AI. Ask AI itself: "That answer was not quite right. What additional information do you need from me to give a better answer?" This is a powerful way to improve your prompts.

Questions

Module 7 — Your questions answered

Do I need to use ALL four parts of RACE every time? +
Not always. For simple tasks, even two parts (Action + Context) are enough. But for important work — marketing campaigns, business communications, complex writing — use all four. Think of RACE as the complete version and use as many parts as the task needs. The more complex the task, the more parts you should include.
How long should my prompts be? +
There is no perfect length, but a good rule of thumb is: the more important the output, the more detail in the prompt. For a quick social post, 3-4 sentences is enough. For a full marketing campaign, a paragraph or two of context pays off enormously. If you find yourself writing very long prompts repeatedly, save them as templates — that is your automation system right there.
What if AI gives a wrong or bad answer even with a good prompt? +
This happens, and it is normal. The most effective response is to tell AI exactly what was wrong: "The tone was too formal — can you rewrite it more casually?" or "You missed including our festival discount — please add that." You can also ask AI: "What additional information would help you give a better answer?" AI will often tell you exactly what context it needs. Every iteration makes the output better.
Is chain prompting only for big projects? +
No — even a simple 2-step chain can dramatically improve results. For example: first ask AI to brainstorm 10 product names, then ask it to write a description for your favourite one. That is a chain. Any time you build on a previous AI response rather than starting fresh, you are chain prompting. Start small and you will naturally get into the habit.
Does ChatGPT save my custom instructions permanently? +
Yes — custom instructions are saved to your account and apply to all new conversations automatically. They do not affect conversations you already had. You can update them anytime if your business changes. This is one of the most powerful free features that most people never discover — use it.
